Who We Are

Built on Trust, Crafted with Precision

Dalco Aluminium Company Ltd has been a cornerstone of quality aluminium fabrication in Lagos for over a decade. Based in Epe, we serve clients across Lagos State and beyond — delivering windows, doors, partitions, and roofing solutions that combine durability with aesthetic excellence.

Our team of skilled craftsmen brings years of hands-on experience to every project. Whether it's a single window for your home or a full commercial fit-out, we treat every job with the same level of care and professionalism.
